Welcome

This is the practitioner lab guide for the two-day IFP Delivery Partner Workshop. It covers the complete IFP 2.0 delivery workflow: App Framework configuration, model generation, error log review, and building customer-specific extensions.

Use the sidebar to navigate between pages. All lab exercises reference the FintechCo fictional company profile. The facilitator guide (last page) contains the full run-of-show, click paths, and debrief answer keys.
