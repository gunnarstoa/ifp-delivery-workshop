9. Executive Summary Report (Internal)

Polished one-pager for Anaplan internal executives and partner alliances leadership. Includes narrative framing, headline numbers, learning progression, partner mix, standouts, an at-risk callout for partners with zero completion, and program-level recommendations for the next cohort.

10. Partner Sponsor Reports

One report per partner firm, addressed to the partner sponsor. Celebrates their team's participation and learning progression, calls out the biggest pre→post improver on their team, and proposes five concrete next steps to translate workshop completion into delivery activity — including copying the reference IFP model + app into the partner's tenant.

11. Export Updated Cohort JSON

Downloads the cohort JSON with your local checkbox changes + uploaded survey scores merged in. Replace the file at data/cohorts/{cohort-id}.json and commit to checkpoint progress for other facilitators.
