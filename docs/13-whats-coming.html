IFP Roadmap — April 2026 Snapshot

The following reflects what was shared at the April 2026 workshop delivery. Anaplan's IFP roadmap evolves frequently — verify current status on Anapedia and the Anaplan Community before sharing with customers.

ℹ Content Currency

This page is frozen to the April 2026 workshop delivery. For the current IFP roadmap, check Anapedia (Anaplan's documentation hub) and the Anaplan Community Partner portal. Roadmap items mentioned at a partner workshop may be earlier than public announcements.

Upcoming IFP Capabilities

1

CapEx Planning Application

"Later this year we're coming out with an application for CapEx projects — if you have a customer engagement in the next 6 months, it will be bespoke for now." A purpose-built CapEx planning application is on the IFP roadmap. Until it is available, CapEx requirements are addressed as a custom extension or a separate Anaplan build.

2

ADO Evolution

Anaplan Data Orchestrator is expected to see continued investment in IFP 2.0 — specifically around pre-built data pipelines for common ERP sources and improved error reporting in pipeline runs. Current ADO setup in IFP requires manual pipeline configuration in most customer environments.

3

Headcount Planning 2.0 Roadmap

Headcount Planning 2.0 (released with IFP 2.0) is being iterated. Planned enhancements include additional planning method types and tighter integration with workforce planning scenarios. Watch the Anaplan Community for release notes.

4

App Framework Enhancements

The App Framework configurator is expected to expand its configuration coverage — reducing the number of cases where customers need custom extensions for requirements that could be handled via configuration. Each IFP release has added new configuration questions.
