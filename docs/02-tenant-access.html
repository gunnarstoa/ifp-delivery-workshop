Tenant Access

Confirm you have access to the correct training tenant before beginning

Getting Started5 min

Before You Begin

This workshop uses a shared training tenant with pre-staged IFP 2.1 models. You must confirm access to the correct tenant and workspace before proceeding. Do not use a production tenant.

⚠ Important

If you cannot confirm tenant access by the steps below, contact your facilitator before continuing. All hands-on exercises depend on this access being in place.

Step 1 — Log In

Navigate to app.anaplan.com and log in with the credentials provided by your facilitator.

📋 Screenshot: Login screen — to be added

Step 2 — Confirm the Correct Tenant

After logging in, verify that you are in the correct training tenant. The tenant name will be displayed in the top navigation bar.

📋 Screenshot: Tenant selector / top nav — to be added

Step 3 — Locate the Training Workspace

From the home screen, confirm the training workspace is visible in your workspace list.

📋 Screenshot: Workspace list — to be added

You're Ready

Once you can see the training workspace, you're set up correctly. Proceed to the IFP Overview to begin the workshop content.
