IFP Delivery Partner Workshop

A hands-on, fully self-paced virtual program that takes you from knowing about IFP to delivering it for real customers.

What This Workshop Is

The IFP Delivery Partner Workshop covers the complete IFP 2.1 delivery workflow — Application Framework configuration, model generation, error log resolution, and building real customer-specific extensions from scratch. Every lab happens in your own dedicated training tenant with pre-staged IFP models and real planning scenarios (OpEx/Headcount, Insurance 3-Statement, Nuclear Plant OpEx).

If you've ever generated an IFP model, hit an error log, and weren't sure whether to escalate or fix it yourself — this workshop is built to close that gap.

What You'll Be Able To Do

  • Configure the Application Framework end-to-end and generate a functional IFP model
  • Read and resolve generation error logs without escalating basic formula errors
  • Build a direct-input extension with adjustment layers
  • Build a headcount-driven rate planning method
  • Apply Configure-vs-Extend-vs-Modify discipline and data-tag every change you make

Who It's For

Anaplan delivery consultants at partner firms who are implementing IFP for customers — or expect to in the next 12 months. This is a practitioner workshop, not a product overview session.

Format & Time Commitment

Virtual. Fully self-paced. Each participant receives their own dedicated training tenant for the week of their session. Most participants finish the workshop, labs, and supporting videos in an hour or two a day.

Optional live kickoff session at 12:00 PM Eastern on opening day — recording posted same day for anyone who can't attend live.

Frequently Asked

Do I have to attend the live kickoff session?

No. It's optional. The workshop is fully self-paced and the recording is posted the same day.

How long is my tenant active?

One week — the week of your registered session. Plan to complete the workshop within that window.

What if I get stuck during the workshop?

A dedicated Slack channel is staffed by IFP product experts throughout the workshop week. The lab guide has the channel link and email-into-channel fallback.

Are the materials available in languages other than English?

Yes — German, Japanese, Spanish, French, and Portuguese versions are available. The in-page language switcher lets participants change languages at any time.

Will the lab guide stay available after the workshop?

Yes. The lab guide stays online. The Slack channel also stays open for ongoing questions when you hit real customer engagements.
