Workshop Overview

Objectives, prerequisites, structure, and how to use this guide

Getting Started15 min

Welcome

You're in! The IFP Delivery Workshop is a hands-on, fully self-paced virtual program that takes you through the complete IFP 2.1 delivery workflow — Application Framework configuration, model generation, error log resolution, and building real customer-specific extensions from scratch.

If you've ever generated an IFP model, hit an error log, and weren't sure whether to escalate or fix it yourself — this workshop is built to close that gap.

💬 You're Not Alone — Join the Workshop Slack

Our IFP product experts staff a dedicated Slack channel for workshop participants. Drop in any time during the workshop week — ask questions, share what you're stuck on, see what other participants are asking.

Slack channel: #ext-delivery-workshop-ifp-anaplan

What You'll Learn

You'll work through the full IFP delivery lifecycle:

  • Application Framework configuration — all 44 configuration questions, end-to-end
  • Model generation & post-generation verification — what generation produces, what to check, and why
  • Error log review — read, categorize, and resolve generation errors without escalating
  • Extensions from scratch — two production-grade builds: a multi-adjustment direct input method and a headcount-driven rate method
  • Anaplan Way for Applications — the six-phase delivery methodology that frames every IFP engagement

Why This Matters

IFP is delivered through the Application Framework — not built from scratch. That makes configuration, generation, and error log triage the core of the delivery skillset, not custom modeling. The configuration decisions, the post-generation checklist, and the data tag discipline for extensions aren't optional — they are the delivery method.

Every lab happens in your own assigned training tenant with pre-staged IFP models, using real planning scenarios (OpEx/Headcount, Insurance 3-Statement, Nuclear Plant OpEx).

By the End, You'll Be Able To

#Outcome
1Configure the Application Framework end-to-end and generate a functional IFP model
2Read and resolve generation error logs without escalating basic formula errors
3Build a direct-input extension with adjustment layers
4Build a headcount-driven rate planning method
5Apply Configure-vs-Extend-vs-Modify discipline and data tag every change you make

Who This Is For

Anaplan delivery consultants at partner firms who are implementing IFP for customers — or expect to in the next 12 months. This is a practitioner workshop, not a product overview.

Prerequisites:

  • Model Builder (MB), Solution Architect (SA), or Master Anaplanner certification
  • Prior experience building or configuring Anaplan models
  • Familiarity with financial planning concepts (P&L, balance sheet, headcount)

Your Training Tenant for the Week

You've been assigned a dedicated training tenant pre-loaded with IFP 2.1 models for the week of your session. All configuration and extension work happens in this training tenant — not in production. Most participants finish the workshop, labs, and supporting videos in an hour or two a day.

⚠ Confirm Your Access Before You Start

Before you do anything else, head to Tenant Access and walk through the three checks: log in, confirm the correct tenant, and verify your roles and dataspace. Skipping this is the #1 reason participants get stuck mid-workshop.
