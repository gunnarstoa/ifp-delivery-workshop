Workshop Overview

Objectives, prerequisites, structure, and how to use this guide

Getting Started15 min

What This Workshop Is

This is a self-paced hands-on IFP 2.1 delivery workshop for Anaplan partner practitioners. The workshop covers the full App Framework configuration workflow — from Application Framework Configurator walk-through through model generation, post-generation steps, and error log review — followed by extension development with two hands-on labs building real IFP planning method extensions from scratch.

The workshop uses a training tenant with pre-staged IFP models. All configuration and extension work happens in these training models — not in production.

Who It's For

Anaplan delivery consultants at partner firms who are implementing IFP for customers or expect to in the next 12 months. This is a practitioner workshop — not a product overview session.

Prerequisites:

  • Active Anaplan workspace admin access (confirmed before Day 1)
  • Model Builder (MB), Solution Architect (SA), or Master Anaplanner certification
  • Prior experience building or configuring Anaplan models
  • Familiarity with financial planning concepts (P&L, balance sheet, headcount)

What You'll Be Able to Do

#Verifiable Outcome
1Configure the IFP App Framework end-to-end and generate a functional IFP model
2Read, interpret, and resolve generation error logs without escalating basic formula errors
3Build two extensions from scratch: a multi-adjustment direct input method and a headcount-driven rate method

How to Use This Guide

This is a self-paced workshop. Work through the sections at your own speed:

  • Getting Started: IFP Overview, Anaplan Way, and architecture
  • Configuration: Configurator walkthrough, exercises, generation, and error log review
  • Extensions: Extension overview and two hands-on labs
  • Reference: Q&A, facilitator guide, and roadmap

Each section includes text, screenshots, and exercises. Complete the hands-on labs independently in your training tenant, following the Tell-Show-Do pattern below.

Tell · Show · Do Engagement

ModeWhat HappensYour Role
📖 TellFacilitator explains the concept and why it matters for IFP deliveryListen, take notes, ask clarifying questions
👁 ShowFacilitator demonstrates live in the training tenantFollow along in your own workspace
✅ DoYou complete the activity independently in the training modelWork through steps, raise blockers, compare with neighbors
⚠ Training Tenant Access

This workshop uses training tenant credentials provided at session start. Do not use production models. Your facilitator will confirm access before Day 1 begins.
