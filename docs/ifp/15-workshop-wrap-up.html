Tenant Cleanup Checklist

The training tenant is shared across many cohorts. Running through these five steps before you leave keeps the tenant in a clean state for the next group of participants — and keeps these workshops sustainable to run.

⚠ Do Not Delete TRAIN Models or Apps

The pre-populated TRAIN models and applications (those with the TRAIN prefix, for example IFPv2.0.0-train-base-financial-planning) belong to all participants and must stay in place. Only delete the apps, models, and objects you created during the exercises.

  • Delete the applications you configured during the exercises — those without the TRAIN prefix
  • Delete the models generated from your configured applications — those without the TRAIN prefix
  • Delete any objects you added to your assigned Dataspace during the exercises
  • Reset hierarchies and configuration questions to default in the Application Framework Configurator (one hierarchy at a time — there is no global reset)
  • Restore the Financial Planning model to its pre-extension ID
    • For Application Training tenants 00 through 60, the pre-extension ID is 2064565
    • For tenants used in EMEA workshops, the ID may differ — confirm with the EMEA tenant admin before restoring
