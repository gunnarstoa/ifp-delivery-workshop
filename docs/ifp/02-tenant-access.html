Tenant Access

Confirm you have access to the correct training tenant before beginning

Getting Started5 min

Before You Begin

This workshop uses a dedicated training tenant per participant, pre-staged with IFP 2.1 models. You must confirm access to the correct tenant and workspace before proceeding. Do not use a production tenant.

⚠ Important

If you cannot confirm tenant access by the steps below, ask in the workshop Slack channel before continuing. All hands-on exercises depend on this access being in place.

Step 1 — Log In

Navigate to https://us1a.app.anaplan.com/ and log in with the credentials sent in your tenant access email.

Step 2 — Confirm the Correct Tenant

After logging in, verify that you are in the correct training tenant. The tenant name will be displayed in the top navigation bar.

Step 3 — Verify Your Roles and Dataspace

Go to the Administration section and confirm your user has all four of the roles required to run generation, plus a Dataspace assigned. Get into the habit of doing this before every generation — it's the single most common reason a partner gets stuck mid-engagement.

Your user must have all four of the following roles:

  • Application Owner
  • Workspace Admin
  • Page Builder
  • Integration Admin

In addition, verify that a dataspace is assigned to your user. Without it, generation will fail.

⚠ Why This Matters

We routinely see partners attempt generation without first verifying these access rights with the tenant admin — and then lose hours debugging a generation failure that was actually a permissions problem. Always confirm roles and dataspace assignment with your tenant admin before you start configuring. Make it a pre-generation habit.

About the Pre-Populated TRAIN Models

Your training tenant also contains a set of models and an application that Anaplan provides outside of anything you configure during the workshop exercises. These are named with a TRAIN prefix (for example, IFPv2.0.0-train-base-financial-planning) and come pre-populated with realistic sample data.

⚠ Use TRAIN Models for the Extension Exercises

When you reach the Extensions section of the workshop, complete the extension exercises against the pre-populated TRAIN models — not the models you generated from your own configuration. The TRAIN models come loaded with data, which is required to demonstrate and validate extension behavior end-to-end. Your own configured models are for the configuration and generation exercises only.

You're Ready

Once you've confirmed you're in the correct training tenant, have the four required roles, have a Dataspace assigned, and know which models to use for which exercises, you're set up correctly. Proceed to the IFP Overview to begin the workshop content.
