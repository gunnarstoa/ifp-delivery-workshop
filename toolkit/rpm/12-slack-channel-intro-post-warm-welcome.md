# Slack Channel Intro Post (warm welcome)
> Use when: Post in the channel as a regular (not pinned) message when participants start joining. Sets a low-barrier, ask-anything tone. Complements the more functional pinned welcome above.

👋 Welcome to the workshop — glad you're here!

This channel is your direct line to the RPM product team during the workshop week. We're here, we're watching, and we'll respond fast.

A few things to know:

🙋 Post your questions. Configurator quirks in T&Q, "why does Segmentation feed Capacity this way?", "did I break my model?", "how would you scope this for a real customer?" — all fair game. Don't be shy.

🧵 Threading helps. Reply in thread on someone else's question if you've got the same one — keeps the channel scannable.

⚡ No question is too small. If you're stuck on a Check My Work step or a CoModeler prompt isn't returning what you expected, ask. We'd rather help you in 5 minutes than have you bang your head against a problem for two hours.

👀 Lurking is fine too. Some of the best learning happens by watching others work through their issues. Skim the channel daily even if you don't have your own questions. The four app tracks (T&Q, Segmentation, Capacity, Sales Forecasting) cover a lot — other people's questions will be your questions soon enough.

Happy to have you here. Let's make this a good week.
