# Live Kickoff Intro Script (~10 min)

LIVE KICKOFF INTRO — TALKING POINTS (~10 min)

Adapt freely to your delivery style. Use these as anchors.

OPEN (1 min)
------------
Welcome everyone. Quick agenda: about 10 minutes from me on what to expect this week, then Q&A. Recording will be posted later today for anyone who couldn't join live.

WHY RPM MATTERS (1 min)
-----------------------
RPM is how Anaplan shows up for revenue leaders end-to-end — Territory & Quota, Account Segmentation, Capacity Planning, and Sales Forecasting all wired together. Customers don't buy these in isolation. The point of this week is so you can speak to the whole suite, not just one app.

WHAT THIS WORKSHOP IS (2 min)
-----------------------------
Five sentences:
1. Four app tracks — T&Q, Segmentation, Capacity, Sales Forecasting — each with its own labs.
2. T&Q goes deepest: an 8-step configurator that mirrors how you'd actually scope a real customer.
3. Segmentation and Capacity sit in the middle — enough depth to handle the integration questions.
4. Sales Forecasting is templated (no per-implementation configurator) and includes the AI Prediction Lab where you'll work through the Forecaster algorithm panel.
5. Your tenant is provisioned and yours through end of week. RPM product experts are on Slack the whole time.

THE LAB GUIDE (2 min)
---------------------
[Screen share the workshop landing page]
Sidebar navigation: Getting Started → the four app tracks → Reference.
Work at your own pace. Most participants finish in an hour or two a day.
The "Read · Watch · Do" pattern is consistent across sections.
Two things every lab has:
- "Check my Work" buttons inline — click them as you go, don't save them all for the end.
- CoModeler on the model-review pages — natural-language model navigation. Use it instead of clicking around the module tree.

SELF-PACED EXPECTATIONS (1 min)
-------------------------------
Monday is kickoff, content opens after this call. Aim to finish by Friday-ish — that gives you the weekend buffer if life happens. You don't have to do tracks in order, but T&Q first is the natural path because Segmentation feeds into it.

WHERE TO GET HELP (1 min)
-------------------------
Slack channel is the fastest path — #ext-delivery-workshop-rpm-anaplan. Link's in the lab guide. No Slack? Email-into-channel address is there too.

KNOWLEDGE CHECK AT THE END (1 min)
----------------------------------
Ten questions, open book, unlimited retakes to hit 70%. It's not a gotcha — it's a checkpoint so you know the suite landed. Most people pass on the first or second try.

Q&A (1+ min)
------------
Open the floor. Common questions to be ready for:
- "How is RPM different from Sales Cloud?" → Sales Cloud is the CRM; RPM is the planning layer on top of it.
- "Do I have to do all four tracks?" → Yes for the knowledge check to make sense, but you can sequence them however works.
- "Can I extend my tenant access?" → Case by case, contact me.
- "Do I need to attend the live kickoff to count as participating?" → No, fully self-paced.
