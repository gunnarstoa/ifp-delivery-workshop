Subject: Hands-On Virtual Enablement: RPM Apps Workshop — Reserve Your Seat (and Your Tenant!)

Hi team,

We're running the RPM Apps Workshop — a hands-on, fully self-paced virtual program that takes Anaplan partner practitioners through the complete RPM Apps suite delivery workflow: the T&Q configurator, Account Segmentation, Capacity Planning, and Sales Forecasting, with CoModeler-driven model review at every step.

If you've configured an RPM model, opened the configurator, and weren't sure which knob does what or how the four apps fit together — this workshop is built to close that gap.

What you'll learn
-----------------

You'll work through the full RPM Apps delivery lifecycle:

- T&Q configurator — all 8 named configuration steps, end-to-end
- Account Segmentation — segmentation logic, scoring, and how it feeds the rest of the suite
- Capacity Planning — territory capacity, coverage modelling, and what-if analysis
- Sales Forecasting — forecast roll-up, accuracy review, and AI Prediction Lab
- CoModeler reviews — using CoModeler to validate model structure as you build
- Inline labs and Check My Work — guided exercises with self-check buttons after each step

Why this matters
----------------

The RPM Apps suite is delivered through a configurator and a set of opinionated apps — not built from scratch. That makes the configurator decisions, segmentation logic, and capacity assumptions the core of the delivery skillset, not custom modeling. Every lab happens in your own assigned training tenant with pre-staged RPM data, using real GTM planning scenarios.

By the end, you should be able to:
- Walk a customer through the 8 T&Q configurator steps with confidence
- Configure Account Segmentation that feeds Capacity Planning cleanly
- Build a defensible Capacity Plan with territory coverage and quota allocation
- Stand up Sales Forecasting with accuracy review and AI Prediction Lab
- Use CoModeler to spot-check your work before handing off to the customer

Who should attend
-----------------

Anaplan delivery consultants at partner firms who are implementing the RPM Apps suite for customers — or expect to in the next 12 months. This is a practitioner workshop, not a product overview.

Prerequisites:
- You must register using one of the links below
- Model Builder (MB), Solution Architect (SA), or Master Anaplanner certification
- Prior experience building or configuring Anaplan models
- Familiarity with CRM data (Salesforce or similar), Sales Operations, and GTM planning

Format & sessions
-----------------

The workshop is entirely virtual and self-paced — work through the content on your own schedule, in any order. Most participants find an hour or two a day is enough to fully complete the workshop, inline labs, Check My Work checkpoints, and AI Prediction Lab within the week.

Each participant is assigned their own dedicated training tenant for the week of their registered session. Tenants get refreshed over the weekend and assigned to next week's participant, so plan to start on day 1 of your week — there's no extending access. Pick the session that fits a week you can give the workshop real attention.
